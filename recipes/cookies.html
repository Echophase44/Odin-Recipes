Cookies

If you're looking for the best vegan chocolate chip cookie recipe, than this is it! No need to refridgerate the dough. These vegan cookies bake up thick and chewy, with crisp edges.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup coconut oil
  • 1 and 1/4 cup light brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 2 and 1/3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. In a large bowl, combine coconut oil, brown sugar, and vanilla.
  3. Add the coconut milk and applesauce.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture and combine well.
  6. Fold in the cholocate chips.
  7. Scoop tablespoon sized mounds of dough onto a cookie sheet and bake for 9-10 minutes.
